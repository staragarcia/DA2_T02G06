Solves the 0/1 Knapsack problem using a greedy algorithm. Sorts the pallets by profit-to-weight ratio and selects the highest ratio pallets that fit within the truck's capacity. This does not guarantee an optimal solution, but is efficient for large datasets.

Final Time Complexity: O(n log n)
